Redefining Looks with Qualities...

Just relocated or visiting Abuja for work or other purposes, dining at a perfect resturant in abuja, should be one activity included in your to do list if its not already there. Abuja houses many and lots of local and continental resturants, while the local resturants unveils the richness in Nigeria cultures and diversities through its dishes, the continental resturants exhibits the adaptive nature and the openness of Nigerians to learn and cohabit with different individuals irrespective of countries, believes and culture through its dishes that exceeds expectations. Here are five resturant, that are known for their impeccable services and delicacy that sets taste buds on fire and wanting more.

Zuma Gril

The best menus in Abuja, Chef Laurent Mathieu in the kitchen prepares a seasonal menu that is regularly renewed, with French and more broadly European specialities, influenced by the continent and for those nostalgic for French cuisine. The plates are neat, the setting chic and spacious. The top of the range service enhanced by soft music, sometimes replaced by that of the grand piano. Cilantro

Nestled in a green setting in the heart of Abuja's botanical garden Cilantro has one of the most beautiful settings in the city. This restaurant opened in 2018. Cilantro is a delight to behold, even the climate seems unique, with a few degrees lower than in the rest of the city, for this lush setting of palm trees and century-old trees that are coloured with multiple illuminations in the evening. The menu of refined Indian dishes is just as seductive. the cocktails are excellent see more

355 Steak House

Expensive card for the city, but carnivores will have enough to eat with an American touch on T-bones, rib eyes and other sirloins. As its name suggests, we come here for meat! A variety of Mexican dishes are also available, including traditional fajitas and quesadillas, as well as pasta and sandwiches. Classic grey and white interior, some paintings and a pretty miniature cellar. Also a beautiful outdoor bar in the arty style. Will you dare the 700 gram portion? see more

Panache

High class restaurant located and situated in the heart of the city Panache restaurant offers the finest dining experience with their service of exquisitely crafted and well prepared meals as well as the best wine pairing options to go along with it.Need a place that combines decor,presentation,ambiance along with excellent service, then Panache restaurant is the place for you to experience all that. see more

Cantina

The staffs is very welcoming and the menu focuses on meats, Mexico and Italy, in an original but successful mix. So you can try the T-Bone, Tenderloin or the "Espetada", the famous giant skewers that are a sensation once served. Italian pasta and burgers also hold up well. For drinks, a wide selection of wines, beers from the Bature brewery and good coffees. a good address! see more
